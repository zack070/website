Dean's Message: Our Guiding Principles

Mission Statement

To prepare transformative educators and leaders through innovative research, rigorous academic programs, and dedicated community engagement, fostering a culture of lifelong learning and ethical practice.

Vision Statement

To be the leading School of Education in the region, recognized globally for producing highly skilled, socially responsible, and technologically adept professionals who shape the future of learning.

- Prof. Manasseh Wafula, Dean, School of Education
